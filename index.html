Latitude and Weather

This project was completed with the intention of discovering how latitude affects different aspects of the weather. The data was retreived from the OpenWeatherMap API on June 7th 2021, and includes 1000 cities.

The data was visualized using MatPlotLib to better illustrate the associations of different aspects of weather as they relate to latitude. The visualized elements are wind speed, maximum temperature, cloudiness, and humidity. This site illustrates and examines the results of the presented data.
